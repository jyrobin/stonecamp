How we build

Parent-shaped

We build what we actually wanted as parents — a clear picture of where our kid is, a small daily thing to do, and quiet when nothing needs us. No streaks, no gamification, no nudges engineered for retention.

Curated, not aggregated

Every link in Trellis was placed by hand. Every prompt in a future Grove unit was tested on our own kids. We'd rather ship five things that work than fifty that look impressive.

Small, on purpose

Each tool stays small. Modest hosting, clear export, no lock-in, no surprise data sharing. If a tool earns a place in your week, it pays for itself; if not, it's painless to leave.

Sister to Pointegrity

StoneCampus and Pointegrity share a workshop and a philosophy: small, paid-up-front, privacy-first software for individuals. We just split the K–12 side onto its own front door.

About

StoneCampus is the K–12 sister site to Pointegrity. Same independent workshop, same philosophy, different audience — parents and learners rather than knowledge workers. Trellis is the first tool live; Grove and Lithica are in development.
